Episode 1 Spoilers : A quick look into Tanjiro Kamadao's origin story

Here is a timeline of Tanjiro Kamadao

  • Tanjiro's family was attacked by demons, leaving them slaughtered except for his sister, Nezuko.
  • Tanjiro encounters Giyu Tomioka, a former Demon Slayer who tests his resolve.
  • He learns the powerful Breathing Technique combat style.
  • Tanjiro survives a grueling seven-day test to become an official member of the Demon Slayer Corps.
  • Tanjiro investigates disappearances in a nearby town as his first official mission.
  • Nezuko awakens during Tanjiro's battle with Muzan and protects him from the demon's attack.
  • With the help of his comrades, Tanjiro drives Muzan away temporarily and vows to continue his quest.
